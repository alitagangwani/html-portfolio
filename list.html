Alita's recipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • milk
  • yeast
  • sugar
  • yolk
  • butter

For the filling

  • brown sugar
  • ground cinnamon
  • cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with yeast,sugar,eggs
  2. melt the butter and add the mixture
  3. add in the flour
  4. knead the dough
  5. transfer the dough in the bowl
